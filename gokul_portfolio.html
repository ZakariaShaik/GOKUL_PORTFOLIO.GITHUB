MY PROJECTS

PROJECT 1


Project 1, titled "The Sunflower," encompasses the successful design and implementation of a compact dual-axis solar tracker. This innovative system, aptly named The Sunflower, achieves optimal sunlight exposure for solar panels by dynamically adjusting its position. The integration of Arduino and servo motors results in a resilient prototype that emulates the behavior of a smart sunflower. The primary objective of this project is to enhance energy production efficiency by maximizing sunlight capture. This is achieved through the automated real-time adjustment of the solar tracker, ensuring that solar panels receive an optimal amount of sunlight throughout the day. Furthermore, the project incorporates a voltage sensor, which provides valuable real-time data. This data is then displayed on an LCD screen, adding an additional layer of sophistication to the system. The integration of these components collectively contributes to the creation of a comprehensive and efficient solar tracking solution.

PROJECT 2

The Digital Sunflower represents a notable advancement in innovation, as it introduces a virtual counterpart to the physical Sunflower project through the development of a Python script. This script facilitates the creation of a digital twin, enabling simulation and analysis of the Sunflower's behavior in a virtual environment. A key aspect of this digital representation involves the collection of data during inclement weather, specifically rainy days. This data is utilized to simulate the performance of The Digital Sunflower and assess its voltage generation capabilities under adverse weather conditions. Looking ahead, the project's future iterations aim to elevate its capabilities by incorporating real-time data sourced from the internet. This enhancement will provide a more dynamic and accurate simulation, allowing for a comprehensive analysis of energy generation potential in real-time scenarios. This forward-thinking approach not only demonstrates a commitment to advancing sustainable technology but also underscores a dedication to exploring and pushing the boundaries of innovation. The development of The Digital Sunflower stands as a significant stride toward sustainable technology, and the anticipation of engaging in more groundbreaking projects in the future showcases a proactive and forward-looking mindset in the pursuit of technological advancements.
